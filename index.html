Your Community Counseling Center Since 1974

Resolve Community Counseling Center is a nonprofit mental health agency dedicated to providing affordable, confidential, and evidence-based therapy for children, adolescents, adults, and families. Our highly trained therapists, psychologists, and social workers offer individual, couples, and family counseling in both English and Spanish.

Teletherapy Available: Access care from anywhere in New Jersey

In light of the recent developments with the COVID-19 pandemic, Resolve Community Counseling Center is discontinuing in-person counseling sessions as of Monday, March 23, 2020 until further notice. We are pleased to announce, however, that there will be no interruption of services to our clients! All sessions will be conducted through tele-therapy. These services are confidential and safe. Office staff will continue to respond to phone calls (908 322 9180) and emails resolve@resolvenj.com. Our intake process is very similar to our in person process. We have a large, qualified and caring clinical staff here to address the mental health needs of the community. If you need help or know someone who is lonely or in need of support, please reach out to us.
