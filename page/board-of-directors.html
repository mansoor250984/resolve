RESOLVE Board of Directors

At Resolve Community Counseling Center, we are committed to providing high-quality mental health care. Our team includes a dedicated Board of Directors and clinical professionals, each bringing a wealth of experience and expertise to support our community.

Our Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is responsible for overseeing the strategic direction of Resolve Community Counseling Center, ensuring that our services remain high-quality, community-focused, and effective. Our Board members are professionals and community leaders with deep experience in mental health, nonprofit management, and community outreach.
