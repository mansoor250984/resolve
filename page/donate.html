Resolve Community Counseling Center opened doors its in 1973 as a drug and alcohol prevention program serving the youth of our communities. It has evolved into an all around counseling center assisting our neighbors experiencing mental health crises with quality, affordable mental health services so that they may achieve emotional and mental well-being. As a non-profit organization, Resolve provides counseling, advocacy and educational programs for children, adolescents and adults alike, helping clients lead normal and productive lives.  An essential principal of Resolve’s mission is to provide low-cost, sliding scale, and reduced fee services whenever possible and appropriate.

We hope that you will join with us and contribute to The Resolve Community Counseling Center’s Annual Appeal with a generous donation, which will help to improve the quality of life of our neighbors. This will alleviate the financial cost for those clients with limited insurance or limited financial resources.
