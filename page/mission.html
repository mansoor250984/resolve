Our Mission

At Resolve Community Counseling Center, we are dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality mental health services for individuals and families. Our mission is to offer professional counseling, advocacy, and educational programs to support children, adolescents, and adults in achieving emotional well-being and resilience.

We believe that mental health care should be accessible to everyone, and we are committed to breaking financial barriers through low-cost, sliding scale, and reduced-fee services. By empowering individuals with expert guidance and compassionate support, we help them lead fulfilling and productive lives.

Our History & Commitment

Founded in 1974, Resolve started as a drug and alcohol prevention program for youth. Over time, our services expanded to address a wide range of mental health needs, including anxiety, depression, trauma, family challenges, and more.

As a nonprofit mental health center, we strive to make counseling and therapy accessible to all, regardless of financial circumstances. Our experienced team of licensed therapists and counselors is here to provide evidence-based treatments tailored to each individual’s needs.

A Healing Space: 1830 Front Street

Resolve Community Counseling Center is located in a historic home at 1830 Front Street in Scotch Plains, NJ. This warm and welcoming setting creates a safe space for healing and growth. We understand that a comfortable environment plays a crucial role in mental health recovery, and our center is designed to help clients feel supported and at ease.
