Services

Our Counseling Services in New Jersey

At Resolve Community Counseling Center, our experienced and compassionate therapists provide short-term therapy for long-term life improvement. Whether you're facing personal struggles, relationship challenges, or substance-related concerns, we are here to help. Our highly trained staff includes therapists with advanced training, extensive research backgrounds, and published clinical work, ensuring high-quality care tailored to your needs.

Individual, Couples and Family Counseling

  • Individual Therapy – Personalized support for children, adolescents, and adults struggling with anxiety, depression, stress, or other mental health concerns.
  • Couples & Family Counseling – Therapy to help couples and families strengthen relationships, improve communication, and resolve conflicts.
  • Child & Adolescent Therapy – Specialized therapy for young individuals facing emotional, behavioral, or developmental challenges.
  • Parent Education & Co-Parenting Training – Guidance for parents on child development, emotional well-being, and co-parenting strategies to foster a healthy environment for children.
  • Anger Management Training – Helping individuals develop healthy coping strategies to manage frustration and express emotions constructively.

Support Groups and Workshops

  • Social Skills Groups for Youths - Group therapy designed for children and adolescents to improve communication, social interaction, and build confidence in navigating social situations.
  • Anxiety and Depression Adult Support Group - A supportive environment for adults coping with anxiety and depression, offering shared experiences, emotional support, and coping strategies.
  • A Wide Range of Workshop Topics Available for On-Site Presentations - Customized workshops on a variety of mental health topics, available for on-site presentations to organizations, schools, and communities, providing valuable education and support.

Substance Abuse and Co-Occurring Mental Health Support

  • Individual Preventive Services - Tailored one-on-one counseling designed to prevent substance abuse and promote mental wellness through education, support, and skill-building.
  • Non-Intensive Outpatient Substance Abuse Treatment - Outpatient treatment programs offering flexible, non-intensive support for individuals working to manage and overcome substance abuse challenges while maintaining daily responsibilities.
  • Substance Abuse Evaluations - Comprehensive evaluations to assess the severity of substance use, providing an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan to guide recovery.

Psychological Evaluations

  • Psychological Testing & Evaluations - Comprehensive testing designed to diagnose mental health conditions, measure cognitive abilities, and evaluate emotional well-being, leading to informed therapeutic decisions.
  • Vocational Evaluations - Assessments to understand an individual’s strengths, interests, and challenges in relation to career paths, helping guide career development or job placement.
  • Mental Health Evaluations - Thorough evaluations to assess the presence of mental health disorders, providing a clear diagnosis and guiding the development of an effective treatment plan.
  • Back-to-School Evaluations - Assessments for children and adolescents to evaluate their readiness for school, including emotional, behavioral, and developmental factors that may impact academic performance.

Family Court Supportive Services

  • Parenting Time / Children's Best Interest Evaluations - Comprehensive assessments to determine the most suitable parenting time arrangements, focusing on the child’s emotional, psychological, and developmental needs.
  • Parenting Psychological Evaluations - In-depth psychological assessments of parents to evaluate their ability to provide a safe, stable, and supportive environment for their children.
  • Visitation Risk Assessments - Evaluating potential risks in parent-child visitations to ensure a safe and healthy environment, supporting the child’s best interests.
  • Therapeutic Visitations - Supervised visits with a therapeutic component, allowing parents and children to rebuild relationships in a structured, supportive setting guided by a professional.
  • Family Therapy - Helping families improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen relationships through guided therapy sessions tailored to their unique needs.

Why Choose Resolve Community Counseling Center?

  • Serving New Jersey communities with expert, compassionate care.
  • Licensed therapists with extensive experience in mental health and substance abuse treatment.
  • Affordable, confidential, and accessible therapy options.
