Workshops

Resolve Community Counseling Center offers free workshops on mental health, relationships, parenting, and personal development. Our professional staff can come to your school, workplace, or community center, and we can tailor workshops to meet your needs.

Interested? Contact us at resolve@resolvenj.com or call (908) 322-9180

Mental Health & Well-Being

Taking care of mental health is just as important as taking care of physical health. Anxiety, depression, and stress can impact daily life, relationships, and overall well-being. Learning effective coping strategies, building resilience, and understanding emotional processes can help individuals regain balance and take control of their mental health.

  • Overcoming Depression & Burnout – Tools to regain motivation and emotional balance.
  • Managing Anxiety & Cognitive Distortions – CBT-based techniques to reframe negative thinking.
  • DBT Skills for Everyday Life – Mindfulness, emotional regulation, and distress tolerance.
  • Getting Through Grief & Loss – Strategies for coping and healing.
  • Coping with Chronic Illness – Emotional support and self-care techniques.
  • Overcoming Social Anxiety – Confidence-building exercises.
  • Healthy Stress Management – Practical relaxation and coping strategies.
  • ADHD Coaching for Adults – Tools for focus, time management, and productivity.

Personal Growth & Communication

Personal growth is a lifelong journey that helps individuals improve their mindset, develop confidence, and build emotional intelligence. Strengthening skills like optimism, communication, and boundary-setting can lead to healthier relationships, career success, and overall life satisfaction.

  • Assertive vs. Aggressive Behavior - Learn effective communication skills.
  • Changing Thoughts, Changing Minds - Using CBT techniques to shift mindset.
  • Boundary Management - Setting healthy emotional, financial, and personal boundaries.
  • Dealing with Difficult People - Strategies for handling conflicts at work and home.

Relationships & Family Dynamics

Strong relationships are built on trust, communication, and understanding. Whether in romantic relationships, friendships, or the workplace, knowing how to resolve conflicts and set boundaries can lead to more meaningful and healthy connections.

  • Building & Maintaining Healthy Relationships – Communication, trust, and conflict resolution.
  • How to Develop a Healthy Intimate Relationship – Strengthening emotional connections.
  • Understanding Domestic Violence & Coercive Control – Recognizing and addressing abuse.
  • Handling Family & Holiday Stress – Navigating family dynamics during gatherings.

Parenting Workshops

Parenting comes with challenges, from raising confident children to navigating family changes like divorce. Understanding child development, social pressures, and effective discipline strategies can make a significant difference in fostering healthy family relationships.

  • Parenting Styles & Child Outcomes – Discover your parenting style and its impact.
  • Navigating Social Media & Parenting – Helping kids manage screen time & FOMO.
  • Successful Co-Parenting – Negotiating, setting expectations, and handling disagreements.
  • Bullying: Understanding Both Sides – Supporting both bullied children and those who exhibit bullying behaviors.
  • The Changing Adolescent – How brain development influences teen behavior.
  • Building Secure Attachments – Strengthening parent-child bonds.
  • Divorce & Co-Parenting – Strategies for minimizing emotional harm and building a positive co-parenting relationship.
  • Anger Control for Parents – Managing frustration in parenting.
  • Positive Parenting Strategies – Encouraging healthy development in children.

Workshops for Children & Teens

Growing up in today’s world comes with unique challenges, from peer pressure and academic stress to navigating social media. Helping children and teens build resilience, manage emotions, and develop strong social skills is crucial for their well-being and future success. These workshops provide tools to handle bullying, improve self-esteem, and foster healthy decision-making.

  • Handling Bullies - and How Not to Be One
  • Making Friends & Social Skills
  • Anger Management for Kids & Teens
  • Self-Acceptance & Confidence Building
  • Time Management & Decision-Making Skills
  • Social Media vs. Reality – Understanding FOMO (Fear of Missing Out).
